## Required Icon Specifications

### iOS App Icon
**File:** `assets/icon.png`
- **Size:** 1024x1024px (minimum)
- **Format:** PNG (no transparency)
- **Color Space:** sRGB or P3
- **Background:** Must be fully opaque (no transparent areas)

**Expo automatically generates all required sizes:**
- 20x20, 29x29, 40x40, 58x58, 60x60, 76x76, 80x80, 87x87, 120x120, 152x152, 167x167, 180x180, 1024x1024

### Android Adaptive Icon
**File:** `assets/adaptive-icon.png`
- **Size:** 1024x1024px (minimum)
- **Format:** PNG (transparency allowed)
- **Safe Zone:** Keep important elements in center 66% circle
- **Background Color:** Set in app.json (`#FF6B35` ✅)

**Expo automatically generates:**
- mdpi, hdpi, xhdpi, xxhdpi, xxxhdpi densities
- Foreground and background layers

### Splash Screen
**File:** `assets/splash-icon.png`
- **Size:** 1284x2778px recommended
- **Format:** PNG
- **Background:** Set in app.json (`#FF6B35` ✅)

## Common Icon Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
1. **Add rounded corners** - iOS/Android do this automatically
2. **Use transparency on iOS** - Will be rejected
3. **Make text too small** - Unreadable at small sizes
4. **Copy other apps' icons** - Copyright violation
5. **Use low-resolution images** - Will look pixelated
6. **Ignore safe zones** - Text might get cut off on Android
7. **Use gradients that don't scale well** - Test at all sizes

### ✅ Do:
1. **Use simple, recognizable design** - Clear at all sizes
2. **Test on real devices** - Simulators aren't always accurate
3. **Consider color blindness** - Use high contrast
4. **Match your brand** - Consistent with app design
5. **Make it unique** - Stand out in app stores
6. **Use vector graphics source** - Easy to resize
