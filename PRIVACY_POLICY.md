## Introduction

Fit&Power ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**By using Fit&Power, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (for authentication and account recovery)
- **Display name** (optional, user-provided)
- **Profile picture** (optional, user-provided)

### 1.2 Fitness Data
We collect and store:
- **Workout history** (exercises completed, sets, reps, weights)
- **Nutrition tracking** (calories, macronutrients, meals logged)
- **Progress metrics** (weight, body measurements, streak data)
- **User preferences** (theme, language, notification settings)
- **XP and achievements** (gamification data)

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device information** (device type, operating system, app version)
- **Usage data** (features used, session duration, screen navigation)
- **Error logs** (crash reports and diagnostic data)

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Payment information (app is currently free)
- Location data (no GPS tracking)
- Contact lists
- Camera/photos (unless you explicitly choose to upload a profile picture)
- Microphone access

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide and maintain** the Fit&Power service
- **Personalize your experience** (workout recommendations, progress tracking)
- **Sync data across devices** (via Firebase cloud storage)
- **Improve our app** (analyze usage patterns, fix bugs)
- **Communicate with you** (app updates, feature announcements)
- **Ensure security** (prevent fraud, unauthorized access)

## 3. How We Store Your Information

### Cloud Storage (Firebase)
- Your fitness data is stored securely in **Google Firebase** (Firestore Database)
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- Firebase complies with GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2 standards
- Server locations: [INSERT FIREBASE REGION, e.g., "US-Central"]

### Local Storage
- Some data is cached on your device using **AsyncStorage** for offline access
- This data remains on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app

### Data Retention
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- You can request data deletion at any time (see Section 6)
- Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days
